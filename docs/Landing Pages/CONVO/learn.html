Learn More About CONVO

Why CONVO Exists

For years, social apps have been designed to keep you scrolling endlessly. CONVO is built around real connection, not metrics. Its sole purpose is to help two people start a conversation—and then step away from the screen.

How It Works

Open the app, see a small prompt chosen by someone nearby, and start a conversation. There are no followers, no likes, and no filters. The app is designed to disappear quickly—because the real product is the moment that happens after.

Designed for Humans

Minimal design. Subtle colors. No flashy animations. CONVO is here to remove friction from social interactions and make real-world connections effortless.

Privacy & Safety

Your prompts are anonymous and ephemeral. CONVO never shares your personal data, ensuring that starting a conversation is safe and stress-free.
