Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

Convo values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. The app collects only the information necessary to provide its core functionality: your first and last name, birthday, current location, profile photo, and any conversation prompt you post. Each time you update your information, the previous data is overwritten so that only your most recent submission is stored.

We use this information to display conversation prompts to nearby users and provide context for meaningful social interactions. Your name and profile photo are attached to your prompt so others can see who is open to conversation, and your approximate location is used solely to show relevant nearby prompts. We do not sell, share, or otherwise distribute your information outside the app.

You can delete your account at any time, which removes all of your personal data from our servers. We implement reasonable measures to protect your information, but no system is completely secure. The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated within the app or via the app store. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at convo.feedback@gmail.com.
